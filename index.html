SHALINI BEHERA

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century who helps a boy named Nobita Nobi in the 20th century.
Doraemon is the main character in a Japanese manga and anime series.

Doraemon's story :
Doraemon has a 4 Dimensional Pocket that contains many gadgets to help Nobita.
Doraemon uses his gadgets to solve Nobita's problems, but Nobita often gets into trouble and learns a lesson.
Doraemon loves cats, especially his favorite cat named Meechan.
Doraemon is afraid of mice and goes unconscious when he sees one.
